Shipping &
Vaulting

At Element Vault Enterprise, your metal is yours from the moment payment clears — serialised, certified, and allocated in your name. How and when it moves is entirely on your terms. There is no pressure to solve a logistics question at the moment of purchase.

01
Three Paths to Your Metal

Every order follows the same first steps — payment clears, your items are created, hallmarked, serialised, and placed in secure vault storage at the refinery of origin. Serial numbers and a vault location certificate are issued to you, typically within 14 business days. What happens next is your choice.

A
Ship It
Commission your own freight forwarder and we coordinate with them directly. Your forwarder invoices you. We handle all documentation, export paperwork, and handoff at the refinery.
B
We Organise It
Let us arrange freight through our own logistics providers. We'll present you with a freight quote for your approval before anything moves. You pay the freight invoice on approval — nothing dispatches without your sign-off.
C
Leave It Vaulted
No decision needed at checkout. Your metal stays at the refinery vault, fully allocated and insured in your name, at no charge for the first 6 months. Decide later.
02
The Vault

All metal is held at the refinery of origin — the same facility where it was cast, hallmarked, and serialised. This is the closest point to manufacture in the chain of custody, meaning your metal has never been unnecessarily moved or handled.

Vault Standards

Third-party audited. The vault is independently audited on a regular basis. Audit reports are available to clients holding vaulted metal on request.

Fully allocated. Your metal is not pooled or commingled. Each item is individually identified by its serial number and held in your name from the moment of creation.

Certified. You receive a vault location certificate alongside your serial documentation. This certificate identifies the facility, the items held, and the date of allocation.

Insured. Metal held in vault is covered under the facility's insurance. Details available on request for large holdings.

Clients may also nominate an alternative vault of their choice — a private facility, a bonded warehouse, or another institution. In this case, freight to the alternative vault is at the buyer's expense and arranged through either of the freight paths above.

03
What Happens When
D0
Order Placed & Payment Clears
Your order is confirmed and reference number issued immediately. Metal is allocated to your account from this moment.
D1–14
Creation & Hallmarking
Items are cast, finished, and hallmarked at the refinery. Serial numbers are generated from our registry and applied to each piece. This typically completes within 14 business days.
D14
Serial & Vault Certificate Issued
Your full serial range and vault location certificate are sent to the email address on your order. Your metal is now fully documented and physically held in your name.
D14+
Freight or Extended Vaulting
At any point after certification, contact us to arrange outbound freight — either your own forwarder or through our logistics providers. Or leave it vaulted at no charge for up to 6 months from your purchase date.
M6
6-Month Complimentary Vaulting Ends
We'll contact you ahead of this date. Extended vaulting beyond 6 months is available by arrangement — contact us to discuss terms. There is no automatic disposal or penalty; we simply need to agree on ongoing arrangements.
04
Worldwide Shipping

Element Vault Enterprise ships to any country that legally permits the import of refined non-precious metals. We work with FedEx and DHL as preferred carriers for insured precious cargo, as well as specialist bullion freight providers for larger consignments.

When you choose to arrange freight through us, we will:

  • Prepare all export documentation from the refinery
  • Obtain freight quotes from our providers
  • Present the quote to you for approval
  • Coordinate pickup, transit insurance, and delivery
  • Provide tracking from point of collection

Nothing moves without your explicit approval of the freight cost. You are invoiced for freight separately from your purchase — there is no freight cost bundled into product pricing.

Import duties and taxes are the buyer's responsibility and vary by destination country. We recommend checking your local customs requirements before arranging dispatch, particularly for larger consignments.

05
Common Questions
Do I need to decide on shipping at checkout?
No. You don't need to make any freight decision when you place your order. Your metal will be created, certified, and vaulted automatically. Contact us whenever you're ready to arrange dispatch.
What if I want to use my own freight forwarder?
Absolutely fine. Simply send us your forwarder's contact and collection details and we'll coordinate directly with them for pickup from the refinery vault. All export paperwork is handled by us.
Is my metal insured while vaulted?
Yes. Metal held at the refinery vault is covered under the facility's insurance policy. For holdings above a certain value, we can provide specific coverage details on request.
Can I visit the vault or arrange an inspection?
Vault visits and third-party inspections can be arranged. Contact us to discuss — we'll coordinate with the facility on your behalf.
What happens after the 6 months of complimentary vaulting?
We'll contact you before the 6-month mark to discuss your preferences. Extended vaulting is available by arrangement — contact us and we'll work out terms that suit your situation. There is no automatic penalty or disposal.
How do I prove ownership of my vaulted metal?
Your serial numbers are registered in our public registry the moment your order is confirmed. Your vault location certificate, issued within 14 business days, specifically names you as the allocated owner of each serialised item. Together these documents constitute your proof of ownership.
Can I sell my metal while it's still vaulted?
This depends on your jurisdiction's regulations around private metal sales. The serial registry and vault certificates make the transfer of ownership straightforward from a documentation standpoint. Contact us to discuss how we can assist with any transfer.
Questions About Your Order?
Our team handles all freight coordination and vault enquiries directly. We're a small operation — you'll speak to a real person, not a ticketing system.
